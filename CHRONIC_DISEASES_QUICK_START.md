## üéØ Quick Walkthrough

### Step 1: Select a Condition
- Browse 12 chronic diseases including:
  - Diabetes
  - Hypertension
  - Asthma
  - COPD
  - Heart Disease
  - Arthritis
  - Thyroid Disorders
  - Kidney Disease
  - Epilepsy
  - Chronic Pain
  - Osteoporosis
  - Depression

- Use the **search bar** to quickly find a specific condition
- Click on any disease card to proceed

### Step 2: Fill Out Your Health Profile
Complete the form with your information:

**Personal Information** (Required)
- Age: Enter your age (1-120)
- Gender: Optional

**Current Symptoms** (Required)
- Select at least one symptom from the disease-specific list
- Multiple selections allowed

**Lifestyle Factors**
- Diet Quality: Poor / Average / Good / Excellent
- Exercise Frequency: None / Rarely / Weekly / Daily
- Smoking Status: Never / Former / Current
- Alcohol Consumption: None / Occasional / Moderate / Heavy

**Medication History** (Optional)
- List current medications, allergies, and past treatments
- Minimum 10 characters if provided

Click **"Generate Guidelines"** when done

### Step 3: Review Your Personalized Plan

You'll receive:

**Important Precautions** ‚ö†Ô∏è
- Color-coded safety warnings
- Medication interaction alerts
- Critical health information

**Personalized Guidelines** üìã
- Dietary recommendations
- Physical activity guidelines
- Medication management tips
- Health monitoring instructions
- Lifestyle modifications

### Step 4: Save & Download

**Save Profile**
- Click **"Save Profile"** to store for future reference
- Access anytime from "Saved Profiles"

**Download PDF**
- Click **"Download PDF"** to get a professional prescription document
- Includes all your information, guidelines, and precautions
- Filename: `Prescription_[Disease]_[Date].pdf`
- Share with healthcare providers or keep for records

## üíæ Managing Saved Profiles

### View Saved Profiles
1. Click **"Saved Profiles"** button on the main page
2. See all your saved disease profiles
3. Each card shows:
   - Disease name
   - Age and gender
   - Number of symptoms
   - Creation date
   - Lifestyle summary

### View a Profile
- Click **"View"** to see full guidelines
- Download PDF anytime
- All information preserved

### Edit a Profile
- Click the **Edit icon** (pencil)
- Update any information
- Guidelines regenerate automatically
- Timestamp updates

### Delete a Profile
- Click the **Delete icon** (trash)
- Profile removed permanently
- Cannot be undone

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Can't Find a Disease?
- Use the search bar
- Check spelling
- Browse all 12 available conditions

### Form Won't Submit?
- Check age is between 1-120
- Select at least one symptom
- Medication history must be 10+ characters (if provided)

### PDF Won't Download?
- Check browser download settings
- Allow downloads from this site
- Try a different browser

### Profile Not Saving?
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check browser storage isn't full
- Try refreshing the page
